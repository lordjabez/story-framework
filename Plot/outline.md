# Plot Outline

## Premise

*One sentence: Who is the protagonist, what do they want, and what's in their way?*

## Synopsis

*2-3 paragraph overview of the entire story*

---

## Structure

### Act I - Setup

**Opening Scene:**
*Where do we meet the protagonist? What's their normal world?*

**Inciting Incident:**
*What disrupts their world and sets the story in motion?*

**First Plot Point:**
*What decision or event commits them to the journey?*

---

### Act II - Confrontation

**Rising Action:**
*What obstacles do they face? How do stakes escalate?*

**Midpoint:**
*What changes everything? New information, reversal, or commitment?*

**Complications:**
*What makes things harder? What do they lose?*

**Second Plot Point / Dark Moment:**
*What's the lowest point? What seems impossible to overcome?*

---

### Act III - Resolution

**Climax:**
*How do they face the final challenge? What choice do they make?*

**Resolution:**
*How does the world settle? What's changed?*

**Final Image:**
*What's the last thing the reader sees?*
